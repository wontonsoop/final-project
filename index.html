Dine-in or Take-out

At 168 Sushi Buffet, we offer the best of both worlds with our dine-in and take-out options. If you prefer to enjoy our delicious offerings at home, our take-out service ensures that you can savor the same exquisite flavors from the comfort of your own space.

168 Sushi Buffet is your one-stop destination for a range of exceptional services tailored to meet your dining needs. For those looking to plan a special gathering or event, we offer reservations, ensuring that you have a table ready for you and your guests.
